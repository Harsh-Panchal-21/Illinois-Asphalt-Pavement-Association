Who we are

About IAPA

Learn who we are, how we started, and what drives our mission to improve asphalt pavements throughout Illinois.

Founded in 1938 ‚Ä¢ Statewide impact

The Illinois Asphalt Pavement Association is a statewide trade association representing hot mix asphalt producers and allied companies. The association serves as a central resource and communication link for agencies, engineers, contractors, and suppliers involved in asphalt pavement design, construction, and maintenance.

Today, IAPA promotes the use of high-quality asphalt pavements through research, technical assistance, education, and advocacy, helping owners and designers understand the performance, safety, and economic benefits of asphalt.

  • Advance quality asphalt pavement construction across Illinois.
  • Support members with technical, environmental, and regulatory guidance.
  • Educate owners and designers on asphalt pavement design and benefits.

Association Snapshot

IAPA has evolved over the decades to reflect changes in the industry while staying focused on high-performing asphalt pavements.

Original Name
Illinois Bituminous Distributors (1938)
Past Name
Illinois Blacktop Roads Association (from 1941)
Current Name
Illinois Asphalt Pavement Association (since 1967)
